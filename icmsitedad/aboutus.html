About Us

I Cleenz Macheenz is a small family run specialist machine cleaning business based in South London. Our customers include everyone from Hobbyist to trade and industry. We make use of both dry and wet blasting machinery, as well as ultrasonic and chemical cleaning facility, this, combined with mechanical knowledge and the care and attention utilised on every job.
